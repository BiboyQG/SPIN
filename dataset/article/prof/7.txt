## News Notes

- 2/29/2024


Illinois computer science professor [Tianyin Xu](/about/people/faculty/tyxu) is a recipient of The Grainger College of Engineering's [2024 Dean’s Award for Excellence in Research for Assistant Professor](https://grainger.illinois.edu/research/faculty-awards). Xu will be recognized formally at the Annual Engineering Awards Convocation in April.

- 10/10/2023


Illinois CS faculty presented a record four papers at [Mobicom 2023](https://sigmobile.org/mobicom/2023/), including three by [Deepak](/about/people/all-faculty/deepakv) [Vasisht](/about/people/all-faculty/deepakv)’s group, with one from [Indranil Gupta](/about/people/all-faculty/indy) and one involving [Tianyin Xu.](/about/people/all-faculty/tyxu) The papers cover topics from satellite networking to human body localization, drone mobility without cameras, and mobile app testing. Vasisht gave the keynote at the first workshop on satellite communications.
